Echoes presents
Beyond the Grave

This halloween, prepare for an eerie and thrilling scavenger hunt like no other!
Teams will embark on a ghostly quest as they follow mysterious clues and riddles that will lead them to hidden treasures. As they navigate through haunted halls and spooky corners, their teamwork, problem-solving, and communication skills will be put to the ultimate test.
So unravel the mysteries, explore new paths and unleash your inner detective to decipher the conundrums that lie ahead.
Be vigilant of all that envelops you, as this occasion grants no clemency.
